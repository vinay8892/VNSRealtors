ABOUT VNS REALTORS

 

VNS Realtors is a reputed real estate agency in Greater Noida West. We recognise the importance of service quality, and we have a team of agents to provide outstanding, personalised, and reliable property consulting to our clients. We offer a wide selection of affordable, luxurious and handpicked properties in Greater Noida.

We strive to exceed our clients' expectations by searching far and wide for properties and solutions that will help them acquire them easily. We are committed to providing a positive and hassle-free experience for our clients. We are proud to have forged strong and lasting relationships with leading developers like Nirala World, and Bhutani Infra, to name a few.

VNS Realtors is a real estate agency that offers support to individuals searching for flats, homes, and apartments in Greater Noida. Currently, we have affiliations with Nirala World (for Nirala Estate and Nirala Trio projects) and Bhutani Infra(Group 108 project).

We are the authorized marketing partner of Nirala World and Bhutani Infra. We run ads for our clients, Nirala World and Bhutani Infra, to generate leads. After obtaining leads (contact details of customers), an agent from our office talks with the customers and provides an overview of the property, price, payment plan, etc. If they are interested, we facilitate their site visit.

After the site visit, if the customers are satisfied with the flats and pricing model, we facilitate their visit to our client's office to sign the agreement (Builder Buyer Agreement). After the agreement is signed by the customer, the builder sends demand letters periodically according to the agreement and the payment plan selected by the customer. The customer then submits payments against these demand letters. Once construction is completed and the flat is ready to move in, we remind customers about the registry and possession of the flat. Here, we are only reminding the customers; the builder arranges the registry and offers possession

In lieu of our hard work to help our customers obtain their dream homes, the builder compensates us..
